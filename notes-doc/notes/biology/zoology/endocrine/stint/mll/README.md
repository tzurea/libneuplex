- Transport medium
- No storage
- Non antigenic
- Low molecular weight

- Growth, Maturation , Regeneration 
- Reproductive activities 
- Metabolism and homeostatis
- Adaptation

- Steroid
- Phenolic 
- Glycoproteinous
